    Grace Karin Short-Sleeve A-Line Midi Dress

    A flowy midi dress is essential to any spring wardrobe, and this Grace Karin style immediately caught my eye. Available in five colors, the dress has short flutter sleeves, a V-neckline, tiered skirt, and flattering fabric tie around the waist. And thanks to its simple style and silhouette, it’ll go with practically anything in your wardrobe; wear it now with heeled booties and a chunky cardigan, and style it in the spring with a denim jacket and sandals. 

    Free People All in Denim Jacket

    Speaking of jean jackets, this oversized style from Free People just launched on Amazon and is the perfect medium blue wash. It has slouchy drop shoulders for a lived-in look, as well as subtle distressing throughout the machine-washable denim fabric. Plus, it has functional slip pockets on the sides and two buttoned pockets across the chest. Throw the jacket on over everything from graphic tees to chic dresses for an instantly elevated vibe. 

    Schutz Arissa Ballet Flat

    The first spring fashion haul of the year isn’t complete without ballet flats, like this under-$100 leather pair from Schutz. Available in trendy denim and versatile beige, the shoes have bow details on top, rounded square toes, and anti-slip rubber outsoles. They’re the perfect finishing touch for casual weekend ‘fits, sophisticated work outfits, and even night-out looks when you’re not in the mood to wear heels — now that’s versatility.
