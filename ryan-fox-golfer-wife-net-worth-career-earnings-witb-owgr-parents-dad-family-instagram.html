    Ryan Fox Biography – Ryan Fox Wiki

    Ryan Fox is a New Zealand professional golfer who plays on the European Tour and PGA Tour of Australasia. He previously played on the Asian Tour and the Challenge Tour.

    He turned professional in 2012. On the PGA Tour of Australasia, he won the Western Australian Open in October 2014, the Queensland PGA Championship in February 2015 and the ISPS Handa World Super 6 Perth in February 2019. On the Challenge Tour, Ryan won the Le Vaudreuil Golf Challenge in July 2015 and the Tayto Northern Ireland Open in July 2016.

    In 2017, Ryan earned his card for the 2017 European Tour. In February 2019, he won his first European Tour event, at the World Super 6 Perth, to become the first New Zealander to win on the European Tour in 10 years since Danny Lee. The golfer won his second European Tour event in February 2022 at the Ras Al Khaimah.
