    The best Tostitos dips are flavorful and saucy. They’re perfect for a party or late-night snack. In general, you can’t go wrong with a Tostitos dip.

    We gathered up all the dips they sell and tried them in a side-by-side taste test in an attempt to find the most delicious Tostitos dip. Here are our very important and scientific findings. Here’s every Tostitos dip, ranked from worst to best.

    The following article contains affiliate links that may generate a small commission to us when you make a purchase through the link. Learn more about how we work with affiliates here.

    Tostitos!

    Avocado SalsaWalmart

    This was the only Tostitos dip we hated. The texture is much more like a mousse than salsa (or guacamole for that matter). It’s weirdly stiff and gelatinous. The flavor is strange. It tastes a little like roasted poblano peppers, but mostly it tastes like nothing. We would not buy this again.

    Credit: Liv Averett / Walmart

    Rating:

    2/10

    Tostitos!

    Salsa VerdeWalmart

    While we didn’t hate Tostitos Salsa Verde, we didn’t like it either. It brings almost nothing to the table. Also, it was impossible to find. I had to have it shipped to my house because it wasn’t stocked in stores even remotely close to Los Angeles. This wasn’t worth the effort of sourcing it.

    Credit: Liv Averett / Walmart

    Rating:

    4/10

    Sporks

    Tostitos!

    Chunky Mild SalsaTarget

    Tostitos salsa is so nostalgic. It is the childhood slumber party snack and everyone’s favorite component of “white people taco night.” And while it’s not traditional in any way, shape, or form, it is delicious. It’s acidic, chunky, and salty. A chip dipped in Tostitos Chunky Mild Salsa is always a treat.

    Credit: Liv Averett / Target

    Rating:

    7.5/10

    Sporks

    Tostitos!

    Chunky Medium SalsaTarget

    I have to be honest with you. There is very little difference between the chunky medium and the chunky mild. Since I typically like spicier food, I would opt for medium. Either way, you’ll be fine if you’re worried about spice. This is a great, classic, jarred salsa.

    Credit: Liv Averett / Target

    Rating:

    7.5/10

    Sporks

    Tostitos!

    Nacho CheeseWalmart

    I thought this would be the weak link in the Tostitos dip universe, but I was so wrong! This is so creamy and flavorful. You get strong notes of cumin. I would 100% slather this all over nachos and have a perfect evening.

    Credit: Liv Averett / Walmart

    Rating:

    8/10

    Sporks

    Tostitos!

    Chunky Habanero Hot SalsaVons

    I’m shocked! This is actually so spicy! To be honest, I’ve never noticed this salsa at the grocery store. Maybe it’s not as prominently placed as the other varieties? It deserves to be front and center! It’s delicious. This is the same taste and texture you love from Tostitos salsa with a wonderful fruity heat from habanero peppers. This will be my go-to Tostitos salsa from now on.

    Credit: Liv Averett /

    Rating:

    8.5/10

    Sporks

    Tostitos!

    Creamy Spinach DipTarget

    Again, I’m shocked! This is wonderful! I would never think to buy spinach dip in a jar. In my mind, good spinach dip should be refrigerated. I was totally wrong, though. We enjoyed this dip room temperature, but I’m sure it’s also delicious piping hot or nice and cold out of the fridge. This would be wonderful with veggies, chips, or crackers. Get this for your next party!

    Credit: Liv Averett /

    Rating:

    9/10

    Sporks

    Tostitos!

    Salsa Con QuesoTarget

    Another incredible blast from the past. Tostitos Salsa Con Queso was everywhere when I was growing up. Parties, potlucks, movie nights. And I get why: It’s delicious! It’s so creamy and zesty. You can eat this heated up or room temp. Either way, you’re going to love it.

    Credit: Liv Averett /

    Rating:

    9/10

    Sporks

    Tostitos!

    Tostitos Queso Blanco DipTarget

    Tostitos Queso Blanco Dip just barely edges out their Salsa Con Queso. It tastes just a little bit better, but only by a smidge. It’s so ultra-creamy. The spice is present without being overpowering. Not only would I keep this for snacking or parties, but I would also add this to my weekly meal prep. This would be incredible poured over a burrito bowl.

    Rating:

    9.5/10

    Sporks

    Tostitos!

    Cheesy Enchilada DipTarget

    Tostitos Cheesy Enchilada Dip tastes like going to Chili’s on a Friday night when you’re in high school. It is fun and cheesy and a little spicy. It has whole black beans in it. Pour it over chips and you have two-ingredient nachos. I can’t believe I’d never had this before. I’ll tell you what, though: I will be having this regularly from here on out. It is definitely the best Tostitos dip on the market.

    Credit: Liv Averett / Target

    Rating:

    10/10
