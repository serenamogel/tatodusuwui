    Cliff Emmich Films

    Emmich produced his movie debut in Gaily Gaily’s 1968 movie. His greatest role was in the 1972 cult movie Payday as Chicago, the flunky driver for Rip Torn.

    He is best known as the security guard assassinated by Michael Myers in Halloween II’s 1981 horror sequel. His latest film roles are Inspector Gadget and Treehouse Hostage films from 1999.

    Cliff Emmich Television

    In many TV shows ranging from The Odd Couple, Starsky and Hutch, The Incredible Hulk, CHiPs, Knight Rider, Night Court, and Walker, Texas Ranger, Emmich has produced guest appearances.

    In the season five episode of Little House on the Prairie, Emmich also appeared under the title “The Man Inside,” a role he called his very favorite.

    In 2007, the hit NBC drama Crossing Jordan starred as Emmich’s guest. In the episode, “Fonzie’s Funeral,” he also played “The Candy Man” on “Happy Days.” Actor Richard Moll also performed the character Eugene in that episode.
