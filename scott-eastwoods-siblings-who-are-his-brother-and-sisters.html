    Popular American actor, Scott Eastwood is blessed with many siblings, thanks to his dad, Clint Eastwood‘s numerous romantic affairs. In his heydays, the legendary actor, film producer, director, musician, and politician had a chunky share of sexual relationships, several of which produced children, among whom is Scott Eastwood. Meanwhile, sharing the last name, “Eastwood” with Scott, are his siblings from six different women. Let’s get down to knowing them one after the other.

    Scott Eastwood’s Siblings: Who Are His Brother and Sisters?

    1. Kathryn Eastwood

    Kathryn Eastwood is the younger sister and only full sibling of Scott Eastwood. She was born as Kathryn Ann Reeves on February 2, 1988, in Carmel, California, U.S.A. Their mother, Jacelyn Reeves, is a former air hostess with whom Clint Eastwood had as a five-year secret affair. Both Kathryn and Scott (born as Scott Clinton Reeves on March 21, 1986) went by their birth names until they made it to the limelight. This was because Clint didn’t initially own up to their paternity.

    Just like her father and elder brother, Kathryn Eastwood is pursuing an acting career. She has since appeared in a number of films including Jersey Boys (2014), American Virus (2015), and Virus of the Dead (2018). She is also a writer.

    2. Laurie Murray (Eastwood)

    Laurie Murray is Scott Eastwood’s oldest sibling. She was born on February 11, 1954, in Seattle, Washington, U.S.A. Laurie is a product of another secret dalliance of Clint Eastwood in the early 1950s. Clint had just started dating Margaret Johnson in the summer of 1953. He, however, could not confine his burning passions to his would-be bride but spread it to a young lady in Seattle whom he impregnated before he could say “I do” to Johnson in December of the same year. Laurie arrived the next year but with her father already married and gone, her mother (whose identity remains withheld) gave her up for adoption.

    Interestingly, Clint’s secret lover listed him as Laurie’s father at her birth. Growing up into adulthood, Laurie decided to trace her biological parenthood, and this clue helped her to succeed around 1993 when she was already 39. From thence, she has maintained a very amiable relationship with Clint Eastwood who heartily accepted her. Despite attending events with Eastwood all theses while the media have been oblivious of Laurie’s real identity. She only opened up about her identity at the launch of Clint Eastwood’s latest movie, The Mules in December 2018.

    Laurie Murray is married with two children – Lowell Thomas IV Murray a.k.a LT and Kelsey Hayford. She lives in Lakewood, Washington with her husband of several decades, Lowell Thomas “Toby” Murray III.

    3. Kimber Lynn Eastwood

    Kimber is yet another product of a Clint Eastwood secret romance. She was born as Kimber Lynn Tunis on 17th June, 1964 in Los Angeles. Her mother is a former stunt actress and dancer, Roxanne Tunis with whom Clint had a long affair that spanned about 14 years. Although her birth certificate recognizes Eastwood as her father, Kimber’s existence remained under wraps until July 1989 when it burst on the media through the National Enquirer.

    Kimber Eastwood is a make-up artist and film producer. She has so far in her lifetime married thrice. Her present husband is actor and producer Shawn Midkiff whom she married in 2014. She has a son named Clinton Eastwood Gaddie (b. 1984) from her first marriage. Kimber also has two grandchildren from her son, Clinton.

    4. Kyle Clinton Eastwood

    Kyle is the first of Scott Eastwood’s siblings to be born in wedlock. He was born on May 19, 1968, in Santa Monica, California, to Clint Eastwood and his first wife, Maggie Johnson. Kyle arrived almost 15 years after his parents wedded in December 1953. He is Scott Eastwood’s only brother.

    Kyle Eastwood is a popular New York-based jazz musician. He has married twice; first to Laura Gomez (1995-2005) with whom he has a daughter, Graylen Eastwood born in 1994. After parting ways with Gomez, Kyle later married Cynthia Raminez in 2014.

    5. Alison Eastwood

    Alison is the second of the Eastwood siblings to be born in matrimony. She is the second child and first daughter of Clint Eastwood and his wife Maggie. She was born on May 22, 1972, in Santa Monica, California.

    Alison Eastwood is an actress, film producer, director, and former model. Alison is also a clothing designer. She owns her own line of denims and ranch-style outfits. She has married twice; first to Kirk Fox (1999-2000), and to Stacy Poitras in 2013.

    6. Francesca Eastwood

    Francesca is the product of Clint Eastwood and actress Frances Fisher’s relationship which lasted between 1990 and 1995. She was born on August 7, 1993, in Redding, California. Despite her age, Francesca has married twice. She annulled her first marriage with Jordan Feldstein one week after they wedded in 2013. She later married Clifton Collins Jr. in 2017.

    Francesca Eastwood is a model, actress, socialite and TV personality. She has featured in several films including Mrs. Eastwood & Company, True Crime and The Stars Fell on Henrietta in which she respectively appeared alongside her family, dad and mom.

    7. Morgan Eastwood

    Morgan is the third and last of the Eastwood siblings born in wedlock. She is the first and only child of Clint Eastwood and his second wife, Dina Ruiz. She was born as Morgan Collete Eastwood on December 12, 1996, in Los Angeles, California. Her parents married on March 31, 1996, after dating for about three years. They, however, divorced in December 2014 after living separately for several years.

    Morgan primarily stays out of the limelight at the moment. She, however, she has child-starred in her dad’s projects, Million Dollar Baby and Challenging (2008).
