    Who are Patrick Mahomes Parents?

    Patrick Mahomes comes from a family with a sports background. His father, Patrick “Pat” Lavon Mahomes, was a professional baseball player in MLB from 1992 to 2003.

    He later started a sports podcast called “The Big Mahomes Show.” Patrick’s mom, Randi Mahomes, has worked as an event planner and runs a fashion line called QuarterBack Producer.

    She is also involved in charity work, supporting organizations like Variety KC, which builds inclusive playgrounds for kids with disabilities.

    Both Pat and Randi have supported their son’s career from the beginning, with Pat initially thinking Patrick would be a baseball player. He believes Patrick’s exposure to a World Series game when he was young fueled his passion for sports.
