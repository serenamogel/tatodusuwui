    Shubhdeep Singh Sidhu, popularly known as Sidhu Moose Wala-who is a great singer, hailing from India and works in the Punjabi music and film industry. His skills are not just limited to singing, yet he is a wonderful lyricist, rapper, and actor as well. He did his graduation from Guru Nanak Deb Engineering College. After completing his education, he then moved to Canada where he took the start of his professional music career and sung various songs. He became famous for his melodious voice. While he is mainly known for the track “So High”.

    Career

    Sidhu Moose Wala took the start of his career with the most popular soundtrack “So High”. In 2018, he dropped his first music album “PBX 1” which reached the peak on Billboard Canadian Albums Chart. Afterward, he dropped another single “47” which ranked on UK Singles Chart. Eventually, he became famous for his outstanding contribution to the music industry. In 2020, he was considered as The Guardian among the 50 new artists. Some of his best songs include “G Wagon” which was released in Canada. In 2018, he performed live in India. Moreover, he also performed in various shows held in Canada. Alongside this, he was honored with the Best Lyricist award for the song at Brit Asia Music Awards. Apart from this, he is a great lyricist and has written various tracks, including “License” which was sung by Ninja. Moreover, he teamed up with Brown Boys for innumerable tracks which were released by Humble Music. Meanwhile, he got the greater success for his best-loved songs including “Issa Jatt”, “Tochan”, “Selfmade”, “Famous” and “Warning Shots”. In 2018, he launched the first soundtrack song “dollar” for the movie “Dakuaan Da Munda”. As he is the award-winning star so he received several awards during his career life. Also, he has earned a nomination for the category of Best Age Sensation Award for the song “Issa Jatt” at the 2018 PTC Punjabi Music Awards.
