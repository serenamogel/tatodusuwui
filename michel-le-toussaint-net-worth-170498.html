    About

    Known mononymously as Michel'le, she is an R&B recording Artist who began appearing on the TV One reality series R&B Divas: Los Angeles. Her single "Something in My Heart" topped the Billboard R&B charts while her single "No More Lies" landed in the top 10.

    Before Fame

    She was a guest vocalist for the World Class Wreckin' Cru's 1987 single "Turn Off the Lights" after Mona Lisa was unable to make it to the studio. She released a self-titled debut album in 1989 which was 2x certified Platinum.

    Trivia

    Her 1998 album Hung Jury charted at #56 on the Billboard R&B chart. She released non-album singles in the 2010s called "Freedom to Love" and "It Still Hurts."

    Family Life

    She married Suge Knight in 1999, with whom she has a daughter named Bailei who was born in 2002.

    Associated With

    She and Dr. Dre had a son named Marcel in 1991.
