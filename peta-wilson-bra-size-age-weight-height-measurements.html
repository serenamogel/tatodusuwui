    Biography:

    Peta Wilson was born on November 18, 1970 in Sydney, Australia. She worked first as a model in Australia and Europe before moving to Los Angeles to pursue a career in acting.

    In 1991, she began taking acting classes with Arthur Mendoza at the Actors Circle Theatre and with Tom Waits at the TomKat Repertory Group. Her first screen appearance was in the independent film The Sadness of Sex.

    She then went on to star in well-known films including Mercy, The League of Extraordinary Gentlemen, Superman Returns, Gardens of the Night and Beautiful. She also became known for her title role in the TV series La Femme Nikita.
