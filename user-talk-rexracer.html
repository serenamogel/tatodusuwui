    Local Bugs & Local Birds

    Hi! I'm new on this wiki. I've been always a fan of Pokémon and each newcoming generation makes my heart explode of joy...and because of this I wanted to signalate you something that I noted repeting each generation. In each generation I noted that there are a series of Pokémon who share a lot things in common with each other. I called them the "Local Bugs" and the "Local Birds".From what I noted the Local Bugs are a group of Pokémon who, hence the name, are all bug-typed. In each generation appear two final evolution of this bug-types who are conterpart to each other. The Local Birds are instead a group of Pokémon based on birds who are a always Normal/Flying type. In each generation the Local Bugs are: Butterfree & Beedrill in Generation I, Ledian & Ariados in Generation II, Beautifly & Dustox in Generation III, Wormadam & Mothim in Generation IV and Hahakomori & Pendora in Generation V. The Local Birds are: Pidgeot in Generation I, Noctowl in Generation II, Swellow in Generation III, Staraptor in Generation IV and Kenhorou in Generation V. I've also noted that the Local Bugs and the Local Birds are strictly related in terms of evolution: in Generation I Butterfree, Beedrill and Pidgeot are in a two-stages evolutionary line and in Generation II Noctowl, Ledian and Ariados are in one-stage evolutionary line. In Generation III Beautifly and Dustox are in a two-stages branched evolutionary line meanwhile Swelllow is in a one-stage evolutionary line. Counterwise, in generation IV Staraptor is in a two-stages evolutionary line meanwhile Wormadam and Mothim are in a one-stage branched evolutionary line. In Generation V, same as Generation I, Kenhorou, Hahakomori and Pendora are in a two-stages evolutionary line.
