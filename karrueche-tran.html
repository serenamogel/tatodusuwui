    Karrueche Tran- Biography

    Karrueche Tran is a model and actress best known for her roles as Vivian Johnson in the TV series “The Bay” and Virginia in the TV series “Claws.” However, she rose to popularity after her connection with R&B musician Chris Brown was made public.

    What is the Net Worth of Karrueche Tran? Salary, Earnings

    Karrueche has progressed from stylist to accomplished actor since commencing her career in the late 2000s.  According to credible sources, Karrueche Tran’s net worth is believed to be as high as $500,000, which will definitely climb in the following years as she continues her successful career.

    Karrueche Tran- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

    Tran was born on May 17, 1988, in Los Angeles, California, USA. Karrueche has Vietnamese ancestors on her mother’s side and African-American ancestors on her father’s side. Karrueche attended Fairfax High School before transferring to Birmingham High School, where she matriculated in 2006. However, she chose not to advance her studies and instead focused on her passions, such as modeling.

    Karrueche Tran- Relationship, Boyfriend

    Karrueche and Brown began dating in 2010 but split up in 2013 after Brown rekindled his romance with Rihanna. However, because Rihanna and Brown’s romance did not continue, they reconnected in the same year. Unfortunately, the pair split up again, this time for real, after Tran discovered Brown has a daughter, Royalty. She then sought and was granted a restraining order against the singer in February 2017. Karrueche has been photographed with various other men since her and Brown’s breakup, including basketball players John Wall and James Harden, and she was also in a relationship with soccer player Memphis Depay.

    Karrueche Tran- Professional Career

    Tran began her career in retail as a personal shopper at Nordstrom in Canoga Park, California’s Westfield Topanga Mall, assisting consumers in selecting what was best for them. After three years, she moved to Hollywood to work as a celebrity stylist, but her name quickly became well-known due to her romantic engagement with Chris Brown. She then began modeling for a variety of well-known brands, most notably as the face of Lady Crooks in 2013. Her career was steadily progressing, and she soon had the opportunity to try her hand at acting.

    Ascension to prominence

    When her relationship with Brown became public in 2010, her name began to appear in magazines, and her face appeared on the covers of those magazines. She was gradually garnering notice, which led to an opportunity to become an actress; Tran was cast as Vivian Johnson in the TV series “The Bay,” starring Kristos Andrews, Mary Beth Evans, and Derrel Whitt, and subsequently appeared in 44 episodes of the award-winning series between 2010 and 2017. Her acting career gradually took off, and fresh roles were presented to this young prodigy. She starred in the television horror thriller “The Fright Night Files” in 2014, and subsequently in the comedy film “A Weekend with the Family,” alongside Marques Houston and Wendy Raquel Robinson. She also had a small part in the action-comedy picture “The Nice Guys,” which starred Ryan Gosling, Russell Crowe, and Angourie Rice.

    Consistent Success

    Karrueche played Chloe in the film “Only for One Night,” then Besh in the sci-fi horror picture “Welcome to Willits,” both starring Bill Sage and Chris Zylka. Tran was most recently cast as Virginia in the TV series “Claws” (2017-2018), with Niecy Nash, Carrie Preston, and Judy Reyes, as well as having a supporting role in the family-drama film “Never Heard” (2018) and starring in the drama film “The Honor List” in 2018.

    Social Media Details

    Karrueche has become a social media celebrity since commencing her profession; her official Instagram page has over 7.5 million followers, with whom Tran has shared details from her personal life. Tran is also on Twitter and Facebook, albeit her following is much smaller than on Instagram.
