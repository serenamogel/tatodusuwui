    In the forthcoming satirical feature film Not Okay, Zoey Deutch will play the lead. The actress is set to play a lady in need of care who fakes a trip away, only to get herself into serious difficulty. However, she suddenly becomes the center of attention, but not in the way she had hoped.

    Fans have been waiting for Zoey Deutch and Glen Powell to return for a Set It Up sequel, but the actress has stated that this is not currently in the works.

    Zoey’s on-screen connection with men like Glen Powell and Dylan O’Brien is evident, but she’s been dating actor Jimmy Tatro since 2021. In February 2021, when Jimmy paid respect to Zoey on Valentine’s Day, dating rumors began to circulate.

    Then, in May, the pair was seen on vacation with Nina Dobrev and Shaun White, who are clearly very much in love. They were most certainly an item by November when he wished her a happy birthday. Both of them grew raised in Los Angeles, so they already have that in common.

He has since made guest appearances on sitcoms like 22 Jump Street and American Vandal, as well as movies like Grown Ups 2 and American Vandal.

    Something From Tiffany’s is Zoey’s most recent film, in which she portrays the role of Rachel Meyer, a woman who accidentally inherits an engagement diamond. On December 9, 2022, the romantic comedy debuted on Prime Video amid rave reviews.
