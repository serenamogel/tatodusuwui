    Meganium (Japanese: メガニウム Meganium) is a Grass-type Stage 2 Pokémon card. It is part of the Neo Genesis expansion.

    Pokédex entry
    The aroma that rises from its petals contains a substance that calms aggressive feelings.
    メガニウムが吐きだす息には、かれた草木をよみがえらせる力が秘められている。

    Trivia

    The card lists "Pokémon Power: Wild Growth" in black text when it should be in red.

    Origin

    This card's English Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Gold, whereas the Japanese entry comes from Pokémon Silver.
