She is also the first female wrestler in WWE history to win the ‘Women’s Championship.’ Aside from wrestling, she appeared in a music video for the band ‘Bowling for Soup’ in 2020. Similarly, she appeared in the reality TV series ‘Total Divas’ from 2015 to 2018 in various seasons.     Alexa Bliss Age And Biography

    Alexa Bliss was born Alexis Kaufman in Columbus, Ohio, USA, on August 9, 1991. Bob and Angela Kaufman were her parents. She is 32 years old and has the sun sign of Leo.

    Alexa Bliss Height, Weight

    Alexa Bliss is an American citizen. She is of White ethnicity and practices Christianity as a religion. Aside from that, she is 5 feet 1 inch (1.55/155cm) tall and weighs 46 kg (102lbs). She has an athletic body type, blue eyes, and blonde hair.

    Alexa Bliss Education

    In terms of education, Alexa Bliss was always involved in sports as a child. When she was five years old, she used to compete in track, kickboxing, softball, and gymnastics. She went to Hilliard Davidson High School and was a cheerleader for the athletic teams. She eventually developed an interest in bodybuilding after graduating from the University of Akron. Her parents encouraged her to pursue her passion, and she spent her time competing in bodybuilding.

    How Did Alexa Bliss Pursue Her Career? Professional Life

    Alexa Bliss began her professional bodybuilding career and was one of the youngest females to be awarded an IFBB (International Federation of Bodybuilding and Fitness) pro card.

    She abandoned her bodybuilding career to pursue her interest in wrestling. WWE then signed Alexa Bliss in 2013.
    On July 24, she made her television debut in an episode of ‘WWE NXT.’ She was added to the NXT roster page on their website after being given the ring name ‘Alexa Bliss.’ Similarly, on May 8, 2014, she debuted as a wrestler on-screen and competed in the WWE NXT Women’s Championship. She defeated Alicia Fox in the first round but fell to Charlotte Flair in the semifinals.

    In July 2016, Alexa Bliss was promoted to the ‘WWE SmackDown brand from ‘NXT.’ On August 9, 2016, she won her first match against Irish wrestler Becky Lynch. She also teamed up with Natalya and Nikki Bella to defeat Becky Lynch, Carmella, and Naomi for the title of ‘SummerSlam.’ On September 11, 2016, she was defeated in a six-pack elimination challenge by Becky Lynch. On December 4, 2016, she demonstrated her table match victory over Becky Lynch.
    Furthermore, on January 17, 2017, she successfully defended her SmackDown Women’s Championship against Becky Lynch in a steel cage challenge. Similarly, she won this title again on February 21, 2017.
