    Kayla Driscoll Dies in Stoneham Crash

    Kayla Driscoll, 34, of Somerville, has been identified as the woman died in a crash on Interstate 93 in Stoneham on Wednesday. Massachusetts State Police are still looking for another person who was driving erratically in the neighborhood at the time of the accident.

    23 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Kayla Driscoll, 34, of Somerville, has been identified as the woman died in a crash on Interstate 93 in Stoneham on Wednesday. Massachusetts State Police are still looking for another person who was driving “erratically” in the neighborhood at the time of the accident.

    Police said Driscoll was driving a 2015 Nissan Rogue north on I-93 around 8:30 a.m. when the SUV rolled over for “unknown reasons.”

    Driscoll was declared deceased at the scene of the accident.

    A dark-colored small SUV, similar to a Subaru Crosstrek or Nissan Rogue, has been sought by state police.
